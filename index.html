Enhanced Basic Attack

Gallagher can use an Enhanced Basic Attack after using his Ultimate. This attack will be dealt in 3-hits; dealing damage and recovering his own HP.

Skill

Consuming one skill point, Gallagher can use his skill, healing any team member by 50% of his break effect up to 144 Break Effect%.

Ultimate

Costing 110 energy, inflicts a debuff on all enemies that lasts for two turns. Dealing massive Fire DMG equal to 75% of his ATK to deal an AoE damage, this also enhances his Basic ATK to an enhanced state.
